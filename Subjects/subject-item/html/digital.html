About the Course

Digital Engineering introduces the fundamental concepts of digital systems, including digital signals, logic gates, combinational circuits, number systems, and data-processing components. The course builds a strong foundation in how digital circuits operate, are designed, simplified, and implemented using modern tools.

What You Will Learn

  • Understand the basics of digital signals, waveforms, and logic concepts.
  • Build and analyze digital logic circuits, including basic and universal gates.
  • Design and simplify combinational circuits using Boolean algebra and Karnaugh maps.
  • Work with number systems, codes, multiplexers, decoders, encoders, comparators, and other data-processing components.
